What do we do?

Thapar Institute Counselling cell (TICC) was set up in January 2016. We aim to help students adjust to this new university lifestyle and aid social them in taking full benefit of TIETâ€™s academic and opportunities. At TICC, students develop strategies like self-awareness, resilience, and skills to manage personal problems and handle situational crises.We are here to address the multitude of mental health problems like depression, anxiety, and stress. TICC counsellors provide regular individual and group counselling and training on topics from stress management, building self-worth, eating concerns, how to help someone in distress, and so forth.
