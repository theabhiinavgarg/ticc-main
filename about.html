About US

Sometimes coping with one or more stresses can prove to be overwhelming. TICC is here for you through difficult times. Against this background, Thapar Institute Counselling cell (TICC) was set up in January 2016. TICC has formed in accordance with UGC norms to provide free counselling to students and staff to help them in performing better in their lives. TICC aim to provide quality assistance to help develop students academically and personally and to help students in coping with stress and taking advantage of all opportunities avaliable at TIET.
Quality Policy of TICC: Ensuring a healthy campus community, making services accessible and fulfilling student needs and vehemently supports diversity and social justice.
