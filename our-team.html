OUR COUNSELLORS
Sometimes coping with one or more stresses can prove to be overwhelming. TICC is here for you through difficult times. Against this background, Thapar Institute Counselling cell (TICC) was set up in January 2016. TICC has formed in accordance with UGC norms to provide free counselling to students and staff to help them in performing better in their lives.
